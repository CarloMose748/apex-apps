About Apex Chemicals

We’re not a catalogue. We’re a manufacturer.

Apex Chemicals operates from our own facility at the AECI Industrial Complex in Umbogintwini — formulating, blending, and dispatching specialty and industrial chemicals for clients across South Africa.

  • On-site manufacturing, storage, and blending
  • Quality testing from raw material through to dispatch
  • ESG waste oil collection with full documentation trail
  • Technical support from our own chemists, not a call centre

Why Apex

We’ve been doing this since before “ESG” was a buzzword.

Over two decades supplying South African industry from our facility at the AECI Industrial Complex. Not a distributor — a manufacturer, a partner, and a problem-solver.

Our Story
01

You deal with the people who make it

No middlemen. Our team formulates, blends, and dispatches from our own facility. That means faster turnarounds and direct technical support when something doesn’t behave as expected.

02

Waste that goes somewhere useful

Our waste-to-energy programme doesn’t just tick a compliance box. We work with clients to redirect waste streams into co-processing fuel — reducing landfill and genuinely lowering your carbon footprint.

03

Documentation your auditors will actually like

Full chain-of-custody reporting, SANS and ISO-aligned quality records, and ESG waste oil certificates ready for your sustainability reports. We’ve done the paperwork hundreds of times.

04

KwaZulu-Natal reach, national delivery

Headquartered in Umbogintwini with logistics covering the whole country. If your site is in Gauteng, the Western Cape, or somewhere difficult to reach — we’ve been there before.
